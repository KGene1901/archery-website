About Archery

Archery is the sport or skill of shooting arrows with a bow. Traditionally, this art was mainly used in hunting and combat; modern day archery consists of mainly recreational sport, competition and occasional hunting. Founded on 4 September 1931 in Poland, World Archery was set up as an international body to promote and regulate archery worldwide. All rules and archery values have been since set by them as a global standard. To date, there are three bow types which are commonly used by archers worldwide:

Recurve Bows

May be sighted or unsighted. It can be considered the most popular bow choice amongst thousands of people nowadays for competition and recreational purposes.

Compound Bows

Though a little more complex than a regular recurve bow, the compound bow is the right choice for archers who strive for power and percision. Aside from competition and recreational purposes, this bow is also widely used for hunting.

Traditional Bows

Simple (no additional gadgets or add-ons needed) and sleek in its design, the traditional bow is a good bow type to pick up if you are looking to have some casual fun. Moroever, there are also competitions held for this speicific bow type in some parts of the world.

Health Benefits

Whether it is done at leisure or for competition purposes, archery does bring various health benefits to those who wish to take up the sport:

Focus

An experienced archer is one who is always focused on the target and not easily distracted. Good focus is beneficial to one in a high-pressure situation.

Strength

Drawing the string, holding tension and holding the bow itself build good core and non-core strength. Constant training will strengthen the body tissues for this.

Hand-eye Coordination

Archery requires you to be able to aim with your hand as it receives feedback from the eyes. This form of coordination is built up from consistent training.

Socialise

Going for events/compeititons helps to build your social circle as you will meet a ton of archers of varied skill levels from all over the world.

Improves Confidence

Self-improvement is of utmost importance in this skilled-heavy sport. Your self-esteem will gain a boost when you notice improvement in your technique and aim.
